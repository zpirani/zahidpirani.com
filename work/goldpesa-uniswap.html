Overview

GoldPesa is a gold-backed cryptocurrency with two ERC20 tokens trading on the Ethereum blockchain. In Q4 2021, we launched the GoldPesa Option (GPO) token — offered at a discount for one week to drive traffic to our decentralised platform. I was responsible for the full user interface, ensuring the final solution was simple, accessible, and scalable for users who had never interacted with crypto before.

The platform evolved into a full GoldPesa Wallet, enabling token holders to deposit, bridge, stake, and swap their tokens on the GoldPesa decentralised exchange — powered by Uniswap.

The Problem

Considering the complexity of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology: how do you sell a token to an average person with no technical experience?

With 5,200+ blockchain startups on AngelList, the space was crowded and intimidating. Our research surfaced three core user pain points:

  • Making the initial crypto purchase feels overwhelming and risky
  • Gas fees on Ethereum are expensive, limiting transaction frequency
  • Users don't understand how to generate returns with minimal risk
uniswap.org / swap

Competitive analysis — existing DeFi interfaces exposed gas mechanics and wallet jargon, causing drop-off for first-time users

Research

We conducted user interviews and a competitive analysis of blockchain products to understand how the best-in-class handled onboarding non-technical users. Key takeaway: to succeed in this market, you need to educate and guide while simplifying. Products that assumed blockchain literacy consistently failed with new adopters.

Solution

A clean, guided interface that removes friction at every step and educates users as they go. The product addressed all three pain points:

  • A step-by-step onboarding flow that teaches users how to connect a MetaMask wallet and purchase GPO with USDC
  • A cross-chain bridge between Ethereum L1 and Polygon L2 to dramatically reduce gas fees
  • A staking contract where users can lock tokens to earn a yield risk-free

  • Guide users to connect their MetaMask wallet to app.goldpesa.com
  • Eliminate pain points that cause drop-off on the swap screen
  • Offer live support to help novice traders through the process

Outcome

Successfully launched the GPO token sale, enabling 3,000+ users to onboard and make their first deposit. The platform reached a $50M market cap at launch. GoldPesa went on to win the "Best DeFi Project" award at Crypto Expo Dubai 2022.
