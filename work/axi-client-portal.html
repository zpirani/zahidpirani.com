Context

Axi's Client Portal is the account-management spine for all Axi clients — the surface where every customer signs up, completes KYC, connects their trading account (MT4 or MT5), funds it, and manages everything around it from then on.

Most Axi clients trade on MetaTrader — the industry-standard retail FX/CFD platforms. The Client Portal is the platform-agnostic surface that gets them onboarded, funded, and connected to the trading platform of their choice.

Think of the split this way: trading happens on MT4 or MT5. The Client Portal handles everything around it — identity, funds, KYC/AML, trading-account creation and connection, communication, settings, and product extensions like Axi Select.

The V2 program was a wholesale redesign of the Client Portal. It coincided with a global migration: moving a multi-hundred-million-dollar revenue business onto the new platform — meaning every existing client and every revenue stream had to land cleanly, without interruption.

My role

  • Led V2 — owned the design end-to-end across Onboarding, Trading-account connection, Funding journey, and Dashboard
  • Supported V1 — the earlier onboarding revamp that preceded V2
  • Supported Axi Select — the Client Portal extension layer (dashboard, leaderboard, promotional surfaces, early ideation)

V2 deep dive

1. Onboarding

The opening surface. Where every new Axi customer signs up, completes KYC, satisfies jurisdiction-specific compliance requirements, and lands ready to trade.

2. Trading-account connection (MT4 / MT5)

Once a client has completed Client Portal onboarding, they connect — or create — their MetaTrader (MT4 or MT5) trading account. This is the critical hand-off moment between "I've set up an Axi account" and "I can start trading."

The design had to make a sophisticated technical step feel like a single, obvious decision: which platform, which account type, which currency.

3. Funding journey

How clients move money into their trading account through the Client Portal. Deposits, withdrawals, payment methods, currency handling, holds, limits, fees.

4. Dashboard

The post-login landing surface — the first impression every existing client sees.
