Overview

As crypto became a core part of Axi's product roadmap, I led the design and launch of Crypto Perpetual Futures — a new product type for the platform that required both a new trading interface and significant work on the underlying design system to support it.

Crypto markets overview — perpetual futures with live funding rates, 24h volume, and mark price

The Challenge

Perpetual futures are a sophisticated financial product. The interface needed to serve experienced crypto traders who expected professional-grade tooling while remaining accessible enough that retail users coming from traditional CFD trading could make sense of it.

This required careful attention to information density — surfaces that packed in enough data for experts without overwhelming newcomers — as well as clear risk communication for a product with significant leverage potential.
