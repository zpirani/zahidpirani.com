GoldPesa Staking Contract

Designed the staking interface enabling GPO token holders to lock their tokens and earn a yield — contributing to 3,000+ users onboarding and a $50M market cap launch.

GoldPesa
Lead Product Designer
Zahid Pirani, Alex Deangeles, Shamik Raja
1 Week

Overview

As part of the GoldPesa Wallet ecosystem, the staking contract gives GPO token holders the ability to lock their tokens for a fixed period and earn a yield — providing a low-risk return mechanism for holders who believe in the long-term value of gold-backed crypto.

This was built alongside the Polygon Bridge and Uniswap DApp as part of a suite of DeFi features giving GPO holders full control over their tokens across multiple chains and financial strategies.

The Problem

Ethereum gas fees are volatile and expensive. For a $200 investment in GPO, a holder could easily pay $100 in transaction fees — making frequent interaction with their tokens economically irrational. Staking needed to work in a way that gave holders a reason to hold and a way to earn, while the broader cross-chain strategy (Polygon Bridge) addressed the fee problem.

Solution

A clear, guided staking interface where users can lock their GPO tokens for a defined period in exchange for a fixed APY yield. The design prioritised transparency — showing users exactly what they were committing to, when they could withdraw, and what they would earn — without requiring any blockchain knowledge to understand.

Process

  • Mapped the staking user journey from wallet connection through lock-up confirmation
  • Conducted competitive analysis of staking interfaces across DeFi protocols
  • Created sketches and wireframes validated with a small group of early GPO holders
  • Collaborated with engineering via Slack to build and iterate the live interface

Outcome

Successfully launched as part of the GoldPesa DApp suite. The staking contract contributed to 3,000+ users onboarding with deposits, helping GoldPesa reach a $50M market cap. The platform won the "Best DeFi Project" award at Crypto Expo Dubai 2022.
